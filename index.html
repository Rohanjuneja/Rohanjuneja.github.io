Biography

Hey there! I am a fourth year PhD student at National University of Singapore, co-supervised by Prof Peh Li Shiuan and Prof Tulika Mitra. During my time here, I have the opportunity to work as an intern at AMD Research and Renesas.

Prior to this, I completed my undergraduate degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering from IIIT Delhi, where I worked at AMS Lab. I was fortunate to work at HESL Lab too during my time at NTU, Singapore. Post my graduation, I spent two amazing years at Qualcomm India, working as a CPU RTL Design Engineer.

In the past, I have been fortunate to have worked with Sujay Deb[IIIT-Delhi], Lam Siew Kei[NTU], Saru Vig[ASTAR].
